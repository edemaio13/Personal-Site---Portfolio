About | Birdwatching Lifelist

Birdwatching is quite the rewarding hobby. Knowledge of the birds around you can create an excitement to your everyday life. As we begin to identify different birds, fellow enthusiasts will compile a lifelist. A lifelist can take many forms from a simple journal, to a smartphone app. As a means to practice my web design chops, I've decided to compile my own lifelist here. I hope you enjoy.
